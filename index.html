Our Story

More Than a Nail Salon — It's an Experience

At Lovely Nails Eindhoven, we believe that beautiful nails are more than aesthetics — they're a form of self-expression and self-care. Every appointment is a moment to unwind, be seen, and leave feeling your absolute best.

Our studio in Eindhoven is built on a simple foundation: quality products, skilled hands, and genuine care for every client who walks through our door. With over 1,600 followers trusting our work on Instagram, we're proud to be one of the city's most loved nail destinations.

  • Premium products only — we use professional-grade gels, acrylics and polishes for lasting, safe results.

  • Strict hygiene standards — all tools are sterilized before every appointment, no exceptions.

  • Your vision, our expertise — we listen, advise, and deliver exactly what you have in mind.
