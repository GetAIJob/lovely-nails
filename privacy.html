1. Who Are We?

This website is operated by Lovely Nails Eindhoven, a beauty salon located in Eindhoven, Netherlands.

We are responsible ("controller") for the personal data we process in connection with our services and this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Dutch Implementation Act (Uitvoeringswet AVG / UAVG).

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We only collect personal data that is necessary for a specific and legitimate purpose.

  • Booking requests (contact form): Name, phone number, selected service, and any notes you include. We collect this to respond to your enquiry and confirm your appointment. Legal basis: performance of a contract / our legitimate interest in managing appointments.
  • Communication via phone or Instagram DM: Your name and phone number or Instagram handle. Used solely to confirm or follow up on appointments.
  • Website usage data (technical): If our hosting provider uses standard server logs, this may include your IP address and browser type. This data is anonymous, used for security and stability only, and is not linked to your personal identity.

We do not use advertising trackers, Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, or any third-party marketing cookies on this website.

3. Contact Form (Web3Forms)

Our booking form uses Web3Forms (web3forms.com) to deliver your message to us by email. When you submit the form, your name, phone number, service selection, and message are transmitted to Web3Forms' servers (hosted in the EU / USA) and forwarded to our email inbox.

Web3Forms acts as a data processor on our behalf. Your submission data is not used by Web3Forms for any other purpose. For details, see the Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

We retain your form submissions in our email inbox for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted.

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Appointment records: Retained for 12 months after your last appointment to handle follow-up queries.
  • Form submissions: Up to 12 months in our email inbox.
  • Technical logs: Typically 30 days, controlled by the hosting provider.

We do not retain your data longer than necessary for the purpose it was collected.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only with:

  • Web3Forms — solely for delivering form submissions to us (see section 3).
  • Authorities — if legally required to do so.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via phone or Instagram DM. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe your data has been handled incorrectly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority:
Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens — autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes. We do not set any non-essential cookies. No cookie banner is therefore required.

If you use our contact form, your browser may temporarily store form field data in session memory — this is standard browser functionality and not a cookie we set.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our website uses HTTPS (SSL encryption) for all data transmission.
